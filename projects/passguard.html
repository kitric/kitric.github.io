PASSGUARD

The encrypted password manager

What is PassGuard?

PassGuard is a simple, encrypted password manager. Sync all your passwords into one app, so you have them every where you go.

Encrypted. Twice.

Your PassGuard data is encrypted TWICE before its finally stored on Google servers.

Sync

Data is synced across all your devices, so you don't need to worry about forgetting passwords

Generator

PassGuard can even generate strong passwords for you, ensuring that all your accounts are doubly safe!
