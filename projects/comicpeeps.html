COMICPEEPS

The digital comic reader

What is ComicPeeps?

ComicPeeps is a minimalistic CBR/CBZ file reader for your desktop PC.

CBR/CBZ files are comic book files (Comic Book Rar and Comic Book Zip respectively) that allow you to have all your comics digitally stored on your device. Windows PCs don't come with a default Comic Book reader, so ComicPeeps allows you to read your favourite comics in the CBR/CBZ format.
