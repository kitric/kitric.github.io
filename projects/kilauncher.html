What is KiLauncher?

KiLauncher is a simple, easy to use app launcher. It allows you to have all your important applications all in one place, without the need of cluttering your desktop.

Accessible

Keep all of your applications in one place!

Library

Build a library of all your important applications, and add them as you install more!

Declutter

Declutter your desktop! KiLauncher allows you to remove all unnecessary shortcuts from your desktop, keeping it nice and clean!
